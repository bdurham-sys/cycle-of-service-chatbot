# Apprentice Recruitment Process

## Overview
This document outlines our recruitment process for new apprentices.

## Steps

### 1. Job Posting
We post job openings on our website and partner sites.

### 2. Application Review
Applications are reviewed by the recruitment team. We look for qualified candidates.

### 3. Interviews
Selected candidates are interviewed. The interview covers their background and interest in the program.

### 4. Selection
The best candidates are selected for the program.

### 5. Onboarding
New apprentices go through an onboarding process where they learn about the program.

## Timeline
The entire process typically takes 4-6 weeks.

## Contact
For questions, contact the recruitment team.
