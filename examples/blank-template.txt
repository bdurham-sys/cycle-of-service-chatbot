- Include clear success criteria
- Have defined timelines
- Specify who's responsible
- Be trackable with data

**Customer Touchpoints Should:**
- Cover the complete journey (no gaps)
- Be sequenced logically
- Include timing information
- Specify responsible parties
- Connect to customer needs
